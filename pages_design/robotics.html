Robotics Club & Team

Overview

throughout my time in high school, i noticed a lack of accessibility to opportunities for exploring modern fields in a practical manner. to help resolve this issue, i worked with several teachers and peers to create my school's first robotics club in years. i developed creative ways to market the club and make it as beginner-friendly as possible.

Club/Team

i worked with my fellow officers to develop introductory-robotics curriculums and fun challenges for the members. i created team-based tournaments that determined our competition team, and we have, and will, competed in the regional competition botball.

Impact

  1. 20+ Club members
  2. Majority of members never had exposure to robotics
  3. 5th place in regional competition on first attempt
  4. President & Founder of club/team
