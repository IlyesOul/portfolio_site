program description

the initial phase of the program is predicting the user's credit score; this is done through a series of prompts whose responses are used to train a Random Forest Ensemble model that will predict the score.

with the predicted score, the program will produce a series of questions for the user to determine whether they wish to invest, how much cash they'd be willing to input, and how large of a loan, if any at all, they're willing to take for their investment. based on their credit score and the answers to listed prompts, the user is given bespoke investment advisory

technologies used

  2. Random Forest Ensembles
  3. Numpy & Pandas
